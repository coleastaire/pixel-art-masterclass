Value

If you master only one fundamental, make it this one. Value is what makes an image read — long before color gets credit.

1 · Value is king

Value does the work, color gets the credit

Value is the lightness or darkness of an area, regardless of color. Form, depth, mood, focus — almost everything that makes an image read is carried by value, not hue.

Master studyDesaturate three pieces you love to grayscale. The value structure alone carries them — usually just a few big value shapes.
2 · Notan

Design in big value shapes

Before detail or color, a strong image works as a simple pattern of 2–3 values. Same shapes — only the value separation differs:

The testIf your big shapes don't separate by value, the image won't read — no matter how much detail you add later.
3 · Contrast

Contrast directs the eye

The eye is drawn to the area of greatest value contrast — automatically. That makes contrast your tool for building a focal point.

Why it worksSpend your strongest contrast on what matters most. If everything is high-contrast, nothing stands out.

What to carry forward
